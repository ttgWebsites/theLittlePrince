Basic Info

  • Title
  • The Little Prince
  • Author
  • Antoine de Saint- Exupery
  • Published
  • First published in 1943, Reynal & Hitchcock, Harcourt Brace Jovanovich, Inc.
  • Genre
  • Allegory, Fable.
  • Setting
  • The novel is set 6 years in the past from an unknown date, and occurs in the Sahara Desert and Space.
  • Point Of View
  • The novel is narrated in the first person by a pilot who encounters the Little Prince in the desert. He also narrates the Prince's travels in space.
  • Conflict
  • The main conflict in the novel is between the childlike beliefs of the Prince as well as some of the beliefs of the narrator, with the standards of the adult world.
